Welcome!

I am a passionate undergraduate student at ENSIAS, Mohammed V University , Morocco, majoring in Artificial Intelligence. My enthusiasm for AI research and innovation drives me.

I am currently engaged in two research projects:

  • Computation of Vapnik Chervonenkis dimension for non-discrete settings.
  • Boosting in Robust Adversarial Learning within Agnostic settings.


I've also designed a GPU-accelerated Gradient Descent Logistic Regression algorithm, accepted at The IEEE International Conference on Artificial Intelligence, Blockchain, and Internet of Things (AIBThings).

Before ENSIAS, I ranked 200th nationally in the Concours National Commun among nearly 2500 top students in Morocco's Mathematics baccalaureate.

My journey showcases dedication to AI advancement, blending theoretical and practical expertise to pioneer innovative research.
